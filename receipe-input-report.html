Meatball Vegetable Soup

Portion Size: 8 ounces
Recipe Yield - Portions: 48 portions
Recipe Yield - Amount: 3 gallons

Menu Item Recipe

Kitchen Station: Soup

Note:

A place to say something, if necessary.

Amounts Ingredients
For Meatballs:
3 # Beef, Ground
½ Cup Panko
½ Cup Grana Cheese, grated
4 Each Eggs, beaten
4 Each Eggs, beaten
1 Tbsp Garlic, minced
½ Cup Italian Parsley, chopped
2 tsp Kosher Salt
½ tsp Black Pepper, ground
1 Cup Olive Oil, to brown meatballs in
For Soup:
¼ Cup Olive Oil
2 #, 8 oz Onions, Small Dice
3 oz Garlic, fresh, crushed
3 oz Garlic, fresh, crushed
1 Gallon, 2 Qts Chicken Stock
2 #, 8 oz Mushrooms, quartered
2 #, 8 oz Carrots, medium diced
3 # Celery, medium diced
1 # Tomatoes, medium diced
1 #, 8 oz Peas, frozen
1 oz Kosher Salt
1 oz Black Pepper, ground
12 Each Italian Parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. For the Meatballs: Make 1" meatballs from indicated ingredients. Don't overmix.
  2. Brown in olive oil. Reserve.
  3. For the Soup: Sweat onions and garlic in oil. Add stock and bring to simmer.
  4. Add meatballs, mushrooms, carrots, celery and tomatoes. Simmer till carrots are tender.
  5. Add peas, salt and pepper. Return to simmer.
  6. Serve, garnished with chopped parsley.
