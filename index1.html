  1. Make 1" meatballs from indicated ingredients. Don't overmix. Brown in olive oil. Reserve.
  2. Sweat onions and garlic in oil. Add stock and bring to simmer.
  3. Add meatballs, mushrooms, carrots, celery and tomatoes. Simmer till carrots are tender.
  4. Add peas, salt and pepper. Return to simmer.
  5. Hold at > 135 °F.
  6. Serve, garnished with chopped parsley.
